blobfish

• Weighs around 9 kg. • Grows up to 30 cm long. • Found in the Indian, Pacific, and Atlantic oceans. • They mostly lay still and wait for food to swim into their open mouths • They feed on small animals, such as crustaceans • Blobfish are commonly called the ugliest fish/overall animal in the world • Blobfish have rough grey skin, which turns fleshy and pink, when its out of water.
