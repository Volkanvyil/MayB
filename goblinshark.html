goblin shark

• Weighs up to 210 kilos • Grows up to 3,7 meters • Lifespan of 35 years • Jaws detach to extend bite reach, while eating and hunting. • Can be found in Australia, Japan, New Zealand, South Africa, Mozambique, among others. • Small skeleton compared to body size, makes its body very jiggly
