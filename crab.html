japanese spider crab

• Up to 3,7 metres from claw to claw and 19 kilos of body weight. • Their bodies excluding legs go to about 40cm in diameter. • Lifespan of 100 years. • They are predators who feed on smaller animals such as shellfish, sea snails and dead fish. They also eat plants such as algae. • Their giant limbs have weak joints, which results in a lot of them having missing limbs as they grow older. • Commonly found near Taiwan, but can be seen all over japan's deep oceans • Japanese folklore called them monsters that pulled sailors from their boats.
