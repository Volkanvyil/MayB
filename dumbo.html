Dumbo Octopus

• Weighs up to 1 kilo • Grows up to 30 cm long • Lifespan of around 4 years • Feeds on small animals like worms, shellfish, and copepods. • Actual name is Grimpoteuthis • Can be found in The Philippines, west coast America, New Zealand, the Gulf of Mexico, among others
